A Muddy Path
The mud here is thick, very thick. Depending on heaviness it allows
different people to sink to different levels. So for example, while a dwarf

alcoves of deeper mud are visible to the north and south.

A Hot, Muddy Spring
Here the source of the mud, seen throughout the armaboro den, becomes
apparent. A huge spring of hot mud pours forth an endless supply for the
strange beasts, which have taken up residence here. The effects of the
sweet-smelling mud seem to be quite powerful, as all the beasts in this
region of the den appear exceedingly healthy. The environment is somewhat
intoxicating. You begin to wish that you could just stay here forever. Of
course, considering the size of the bodyprints near the spring, you may
just get your wish. The mud from the spring spills down into the area
below.
