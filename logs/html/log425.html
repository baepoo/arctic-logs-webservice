edge of the mighty white tundra. The snow blows hard across the plains and

back into your cloak to try and remain a little warmer. Your cheeks have

450H 92V 3823021X 0C Mem:4 Exits:NS> sOn the Tundra
The cold arctic wind blows heavy across the frozen tundra, and you feel
numbness creeping into your bones. All around you the snow blows and whisks
by in small eddies and whirlwinds as it makes its way across the white
plains. The snow beneath your feet is very thick and slows your movements
as you try to keep awake in the cold and monotany of walking for hours
without any change of scenery. You begin to wonder if you'll ever see an
end to the white tundra.
